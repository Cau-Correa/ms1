About

Ross Nevin is a versatile session musician and recording artist based out of Dublin, Ireland.
This talented multi-instrumentalist is able to effortlessly deliver quality performances on instruments as diverse as sax, vocals, flute and piano.
Ross also writes his own material: "Awake at Night" is the title of his debut EP, a release featuring 4 songs with a sound that falls somewhere in between jazz, progressive and pop.
Tracks such as "In The Dust" bridge the gaps between the groove of Miles Davis and the melodic sensibility and folky vibes of Wilco, creating a really unique compromise.
